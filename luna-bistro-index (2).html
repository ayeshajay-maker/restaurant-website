Our Story

A Table Set Under the Moon

Luna Bistro began as a single candlelit room and a chef who believed dinner should feel like an occasion. Today, we source the morning's best produce, age our own meats, and pair every plate with a wine chosen just for it. Every evening at Luna is built around one idea: slow down, and taste something remarkable.

From our open kitchen to the last pour of dessert wine, our team treats every guest like the only reservation of the night.
